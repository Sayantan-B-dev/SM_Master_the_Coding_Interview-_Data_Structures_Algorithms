# Resume Preparation Resources & Guidelines

## 1. Objective

The goal is to create a **high-quality, structured resume** by:

* Following a proven checklist
* Learning best practices from expert resources
* Using pre-designed templates for efficiency

---

## 2. Step-by-Step Resume Building Process

### 2.1 Use a Resume Cheatsheet

* Review the provided cheatsheet carefully

* Ensure all checklist items are completed:

  * Contact Information
  * Skills
  * Projects / Experience
  * Education
  * Achievements

* Once completed → proceed to the next learning module/video

---

### 2.2 Study Resume Best Practices

* Read the recommended article (developer-focused but universally applicable)
* Key learnings typically include:

  * Writing concise bullet points
  * Using action verbs
  * Quantifying impact (e.g., “Improved performance by 30%”)
  * Avoiding unnecessary content

---

### 2.3 Review Sample Resumes

* Analyze successful resumes to understand:

  * Structure and layout
  * Section prioritization
  * Presentation of technical skills
* Focus on:

  * Clarity
  * Readability
  * Relevance

## 4. ATS (Applicant Tracking System) Resumes

### 4.1 What is ATS?

* ATS = software used by companies to filter resumes automatically
* Parses resumes based on:

  * Keywords
  * Formatting
  * Structure

---

### 4.2 ATS-Compliant Resume Characteristics

* Simple layout (no complex graphics)
* Standard fonts
* Proper headings
* Keyword optimization based on job description

---

### 4.3 Pros and Cons

**Advantages:**

* Helps pass automated screening
* Increases chances of reaching recruiters

**Disadvantages:**

* Can look generic
* May reduce uniqueness and personal branding
* High competition → easier to get filtered out later

---

### 4.4 Recommendation

* ATS resumes are optional
* Better approach:

  * Combine **clarity + uniqueness**
  * Focus on **direct outreach and strong projects**
  * Build a resume that is both **machine-readable and human-impressive**

---

## 5. Key Takeaways

* Follow a structured checklist before finalizing your resume
* Learn from high-quality examples and expert advice
* Use pre-built templates to save time and maintain design quality
* Do not rely solely on ATS optimization—focus on standing out
* Prioritize clarity, impact, and relevance in every section
